## ğŸ’¡ PRO TIPS FOR ICONS IN POWERPOINT

### **Best Practices**

1. **Consistency**:
   - Use same icon style throughout (all emoji or all downloaded)
   - Keep sizes consistent (usually 48pt or 72pt)

2. **Visibility**:
   - Test from 10 feet away
   - Icons should be clear and recognizable
   - Don't use icons that are too detailed

3. **Color**:
   - Use official brand colors when possible
   - Maintain contrast with background
   - Keep color palette consistent

4. **Size**:
   - Title slides: 72pt+
   - Content slides: 48pt
   - Small icons in text: 24pt
   - Diagrams: 36-48pt

5. **Placement**:
   - Left-align with text or center
   - Leave white space around icons
   - Group icon + text together
